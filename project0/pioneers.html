Pioneers of Drag

The old pioneers of drag have contributed to its culture and have paved the way for the modern drag queens. They fought for queer rights (Flawless Sabirna and Marsha P. Johnson) or they helped making drag mainstream (RuPaul).

The modern pioneers of drag are contributing to this art by creating music and appearing on music charts (pabllo Vittar) or by appearing on television shows (Shangela and Valentina have both appeared on RuPaul's Drag Race).
