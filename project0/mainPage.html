The world of drag!


The art of drag has always been present in society. During Shakespeare's times, women could not perform in theatre plays, so they hired male actors to dress up like women and play their parts. Since then, drag has evolved and drag queens are more mainstream than before. We now have two drag shows: RuPaul's Drag Race and The Boulet Brothers' Dragula. Between these two, RuPaul's Drag Race is more mainstream and popular.

The goal of this project is to give you a small introduction to the world of drag. Please select from the following topics.
